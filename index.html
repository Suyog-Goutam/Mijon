Frequently Asked Questions

Want to know more before you book? Here are the most common questions our patients ask.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

During your first visit, our specialists perform a comprehensive skin analysis and consultation to understand your goals and recommend a personalized plan.

Are laser treatments painful?

We use state-of-the-art cooling technology to minimize discomfort. Most patients describe it as a mild snapping sensation, which is completely tolerable.

How long does recovery typically take?

Downtime varies depending on the procedure. Facials and mild peels have no downtime, while advance laser or surgery may require 3 to 7 days of mild redness and care.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book by clicking the "Book a Visit" button, calling our numbers directly, or sending a message through our Contact page. Walk-ins are also welcome subject to availability.
